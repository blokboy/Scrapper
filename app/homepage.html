Scrapper - The Ethereum Blockchain Scrapbook

Post pictures to your personal scrapbook, and share with others as you'd like :)

How To Get Started...

1. You have to use a Web3 Enabled Browser (I highly recommend Metamask).

2. Set up your account and make sure to connect to Ethereum Mainnet.

3. Put some ETH into your Metamask Wallet.

...You're Almost There

4. Click that Scrapper or Add A Photo button up there ^ and you're in!

What Is Scrapper?

Scrapper is a chance to see just how valuable validation can be.

Aside from trying to quantify what kind of market dynamics engulf social media applications, this was a good chance for me to be able to keep up a personal photo album that I can share with family members and other friends.

Closing Thoughts

We can't remove images from the blockchain (it's going to be there forever *hint*), but we can stop images from being visible. So... have fun... but not too much fun.

Why Use Scrapper?

Scrapper provides a decentralized platform for users to post pictures and artwork for the public to see.

For each of the posts that you choose to share with the world, you can receive a like similarly to most applications already in existence.

However, with Scrapper, when a user likes another's post -- a "tip" must be submitted (as little as $.10 or as high as you'd like to tip the artist). The smart contracts responsible for making all of this possible then transfer that tip to the original curator of the post. So, the better your posts, the better your returns (in theory)!

All of this happens pseudononymously and users may like a photo as many times as they'd like (another contrast from most major applications).
